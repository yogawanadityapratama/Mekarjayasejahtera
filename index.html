About Our Company

PT. Mekar Jaya Sejahtera is a company engaged in the mining and management of sandstone on the slopes of Mount Merapi. We were founded in 2016, and have distributed our materials to several large companies.

PT. Mekar Jaya Sejahtera merupakan perusahaan yang bergerak di bidang penambangan dan pengelolaan batupasir di lereng Gunung Merapi. Kami didirikan pada tahun 2016, dan telah mendistribusikan materi kami ke beberapa perusahaan besar.
Our Featured Products


Batu Split Kerikil

Gravel split stone is the most common type of split stone. Gravel split stones vary in size, from 2 mm to 25 mm. Split gravel is often used for road pavement, foundations and other building materials. The size of gravel split stones ranges from 2 mm to 25 mm.


Batu Split Sirtu

Sirtu split stone is a type of split stone made from a mixture of gravel and sand. Sirtu split stone has a more uniform size than gravel split stone, and is often used for road pavement and other building materials. Sirtu split stone sizes range from 5 mm to 25 mm.


Batu Split Macadam

Macadam split stone is a type of split stone made from a mixture of split gravel, sand and cement. Macadam split stone has a more uniform size than gravel split stone, and is often used for road pavement and other building materials. The size of split macadam stones ranges from 5 mm to 25 mm.


Batu Split Batu

Split stone is a type of split stone made from crushed natural stone. Split stone rocks vary in size and shape, and are often used for decorative applications. The size of split stone varies, depending on the type of natural stone used.
We also provide
products other than split stone


Sand

We mine sand with permits and certification, the quality of our sand is guaranteed. Suitable for a variety of construction applications, including foundations, concrete, plaster and floors.


Stone

Stone directly from the mine is suitable for building foundations because of its strength and durability. Compacted stone can withstand heavy building loads.


Concrete Brick

We provide various types and kinds of quality bricks, suitable for use in making building walls, both non-structural and structural walls. Non-structural wall bricks function to close and provide privacy to the building.


Stone Sand

We also have stone sand, suitable for use as backfill to make the ground level or used in various construction applications. Sand is used as filler and aggregate, while stone is used as a structural and decorative material.


Rock Ash

A versatile material that can be used in a variety of applications. Rock ash has low cost, wide availability, and beneficial properties, which make it a popular choice for a variety of construction applications.
